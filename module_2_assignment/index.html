The Flow of Time

Past

The past is where it all began. It carries the stories, lessons, and experiences that shaped the path ahead. Every success and failure added to the foundation, building the strength and wisdom to move forward. It reminds us of where we came from, the people who influenced us, and the struggles that taught us resilience. Though it cannot be changed, it serves as a compass, guiding us with the wisdom of experience.

Future

The future is unwritten, filled with possibilities and dreams waiting to be realized. It represents hope, growth, and the vision of a better tomorrow. What we choose today becomes the foundation of the future we build. It is a canvas of endless opportunity, shaped by courage, persistence, and imagination. Though uncertain, it is also inspiring, reminding us that every step forward is a chance to create the life we aspire to live.
